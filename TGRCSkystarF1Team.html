About Us:

TGRC SkystarRacing F1 team is a Chinese racing team dedicates to one of the most extreme and high-tech sporst in the world——Formula One(F1). Founded in 2012 and the headquarters located in Taiyuan, China, the team has achieved many F1 awards and ranked in the front of the ranking list.

TGRC SkystarRacing F1 team is founded by two Chinese automobile manufacturer giants' high-level racing department: Techcreate Global Racing Corperation(TGRC) of TCAUTO and SkystarRacing of SkyStar Group. The racingcar, which is named as ROVEY, is designed by LRCdesign and manufactured by TCRT. The power unit(FE-CEL series)is produced by Evocation TMAX Power and Skystar X-Term Project.
